    About

    I am an experienced psychodynamic psychotherapist with a background in culture, arts and economics. I have worked with a wide range of issues such as anxiety, depression, trauma, ADHD, eating disorders, emotional and sexual abuse and suicidal feelings. I also have experience with social and cultural differences, problems with a sense of identity and belonging, inter-generational trauma and FGM.

    I hold a postgraduate degree in Psychodynamic Psychotherapy from WPF Therapy, Roehampton University, a Foundation Course in Psychoanalysis at the Institute of Psychoanalysis in London, Certificates in Trauma Therapy and Safeguarding and worked as an associate Psychotherapist with young people and students at Roehampton University.

    I offer both long-term, open-ended work and short-term therapy, which usually lasts 12 weeks. Sessions can be online, or in person a 94A East Hill, Wandsworth, London SW18 2HF I am available on weekdays, daytime and evening.




    Fees

    At my practice, I offer both open-ended and 12 week time-limited therapy. The fee for open-ended therapy is £70 per session, which allows for a more flexible and ongoing therapeutic process.

    For those seeking a more structured and focused approach, I offer a time-limited therapy at a fee of £85 per session.

    Both options will require an initial 90-minute session at a cost of £85, during which we will discuss your therapeutic goals and needs in greater detail. Please feel free to contact me to discuss which option may be the best fit for you, and to schedule your initial session.


    Accreditation

    As a professional therapist, I adhere to the codes of ethics and practice of my professional standards authority registers, which include the Foundation for Psychotherapy and Counseling (FPC), United Kingdom Council for Psychotherapy (UKCP), and the British Association for Counseling and Psychotherapy (BCP).

    These codes ensure that I uphold the highest standards of ethical conduct and professional practice in my work with clients, and I am committed to providing a safe, confidential, and supportive therapeutic environment.
